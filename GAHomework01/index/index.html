About Matthew

Matthew is a writer and editor, based in London since 2004. He is familiar with all 26 letters of the British-English alphabet and several of the Scandinavian letters that have become trendy in the last five years.

His current practise involves typing – for fun and for profit – and publishing words using several tools and services (Keynote, TinyLetter, Twitter and the comments field in Instagram to name but a few). He deletes more than he publishes, and considers this a good thing.

He lives with his succulents and a modest collection of Lego minifigures. He likes coffee and he likes spilling coffee down himself.
